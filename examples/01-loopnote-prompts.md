## Social Post

Title: `Recruiters Should Not Be Writing Notes by Hand`

Prompt:

```text
Write a concise LinkedIn post announcing an AI tool called LoopNote that turns live interviews into structured summaries, candidate highlights, and next-step actions. Target in-house recruiters and hiring managers at growing startups. Keep the tone sharp, credible, and product-led. End with a soft CTA to try the product.
```

## Blog Post

Title: `Why Recruiting Teams Need Structured Interview Summaries, Not More Tabs`

Prompt:

```text
Write a blog post for HR and talent teams about why manual interview notes create delays, bias, and missed follow-ups. Use LoopNote as the example product. Cover the problem, what a better workflow looks like, the business impact, and practical advice for teams adopting AI in recruiting. Make it clear, scannable, and editorial rather than salesy.
```

## Landing Page

Title: `LoopNote Turns Interviews Into Clean, Actionable Hiring Summaries`

Prompt:

```text
Create a high-conversion landing page for LoopNote, an AI tool for recruiters and hiring managers. The audience is startup recruiting teams that run many interviews each week and need structured summaries fast. Include a strong hero, a feature section covering live note capture, automatic summaries, key candidate signals, and shareable hiring packets, a section on outcomes like faster debriefs and better consistency, a short trust/proof section, a comparison against manual note-taking, and a CTA section. Tone should feel modern, efficient, and trustworthy. Emphasize time saved, cleaner collaboration, and better hiring decisions.
```
