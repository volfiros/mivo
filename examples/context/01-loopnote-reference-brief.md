## Core Concept

LoopNote is an AI interview notes tool for recruiting teams. It captures live interview conversations, turns them into structured summaries, highlights important candidate signals, and generates next-step action items for hiring teams.

The product is designed for in-house recruiters, recruiting coordinators, hiring managers, and talent leads at startups and growth-stage companies. The main promise is simple: less admin after every interview, more consistency across the hiring process, and faster decisions without losing nuance.

## The Problem

Most recruiting teams still rely on fragmented interview notes. Recruiters take notes in docs, scorecards, ATS comments, sticky notes, or not at all. Hiring managers often leave interviews with vague impressions instead of clear evidence. Debriefs take longer because the team has to reconstruct what was said after the fact.

Common pain points:

- Interview notes are incomplete or inconsistent across interviewers.
- Important candidate details get lost between the interview and the debrief.
- Recruiters spend too much time cleaning up notes instead of moving candidates forward.
- Hiring teams struggle to compare candidates fairly when feedback quality varies.
- Follow-up actions, such as references, panel alignment, or next-round planning, are often delayed.

The result is a slower hiring process, weaker candidate experience, and lower confidence in final decisions.

## Product Summary

LoopNote helps recruiting teams create a reliable record of every interview. It supports live note capture during interviews and converts raw conversation into structured output that is easier to review, share, and act on.

LoopNote is built around four core capabilities:

1. Live note capture during interviews.
2. Automatic interview summaries after the conversation ends.
3. Candidate signal extraction, including strengths, concerns, and open questions.
4. Shareable hiring packets that make debriefs faster and clearer.

## Key Features

### 1. Live Note Capture

Interviewers can take lightweight notes in real time without worrying about writing a polished summary themselves. The experience is meant to reduce note-taking pressure and help the interviewer stay present in the conversation.

Benefits:

- Lower cognitive load during live interviews.
- Fewer missed details.
- Cleaner raw material for post-interview summaries.

### 2. Automatic Interview Summaries

After the interview, LoopNote turns rough notes and conversation details into a structured recap. The recap is easier to scan than a page of raw notes and helps teams prepare for debriefs more quickly.

Summary outputs may include:

- A concise overview of the conversation.
- Candidate strengths.
- Candidate concerns or risks.
- Key examples mentioned during the interview.
- Recommended follow-up areas for the next round.

### 3. Candidate Signal Extraction

LoopNote identifies meaningful patterns from the interview, not just a transcript-style recap. It helps teams pull out evidence that matters for hiring decisions.

Examples of candidate signals:

- Communication clarity.
- Relevant domain experience.
- Problem-solving approach.
- Leadership or collaboration indicators.
- Gaps, risks, or unresolved questions.

### 4. Shareable Hiring Packets

Each interview can be turned into a clean, shareable summary for the rest of the hiring team. This helps recruiters bring hiring managers, panel members, and executives into the same context quickly.

Benefits:

- Faster debrief preparation.
- Better interviewer alignment.
- Reduced repetition across recruiting ops.
- Stronger documentation of hiring decisions.

## Typical Use Cases

### Startup Recruiting Team

A five-person talent team is scaling from 70 to 150 employees in one year. They run a high volume of interviews each week and struggle with inconsistent scorecards. LoopNote helps them capture cleaner notes, standardize summaries, and keep debriefs moving.

### Hiring Manager Collaboration

A hiring manager is involved in several loops but does not have time to read long interview notes. LoopNote provides concise summaries with clear strengths, concerns, and next steps so the manager can make faster decisions.

### Recruiting Operations

A recruiting ops lead wants a more repeatable system for documenting interviews and preserving context across panel stages. LoopNote creates a more uniform feedback layer without forcing every interviewer into the same manual writing workflow.

## Objections To Address

### "We already have an ATS."

An ATS stores process data, but it does not automatically create high-quality interview summaries or improve note quality during live interviews.

### "Our interviewers already take notes."

Most teams take notes, but the quality and format vary widely. LoopNote improves consistency and reduces the work required after the interview ends.

### "We do not want another tool."

LoopNote is meant to remove repetitive admin from an existing workflow, not create new process overhead.

### "Will this flatten nuance?"

The goal is not to oversimplify. The goal is to preserve important details while making them easier to review and act on.
